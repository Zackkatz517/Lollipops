Our Story

Lollipops exciting pick n mix concept was pioneered by Laurie Davidoff, the founder of Sweets From Heaven in 1988 .
Laurie opened his first Sweets from Heaven store in 1988, in Smal Street Mall , in downtown Johannesburg .
This novel and delectable pick n mix idea was so successful, that Laurie went on to open many more stores in South Africa and around the world - including the USA , Australia, UK , Canada, Mauritius and other international locations.

Now , we are proud to present to you our newest addition, the Lollipops online Pick n mix store .
